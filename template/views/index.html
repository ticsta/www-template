Static Website Platform as a Service

Ticsta is a powerful platform for creating complex Static Websites.

Features

No Servers
You don't have to worry about infrastructure: servers, databases, software, etc.
No performance limits
Your website has 1 million visitors a day? Congratulations!
No complex programming languages
You don't need to know PHP, Java, C# or another programming language, but Javascript.
Amazon S3 storage
All assets and generated content is stored on S3.
Amazon CloudFront CDN
Website's generated content(html, xml, json, etc.) is delivered by CloudFront.
Low pricing
One of the most important goal of Ticsta is low pricing.

Pricing

We are still working on a production version, so you can try Ticsta for free.

Plans will be based on website's popularity.

Example: For a website with 1 million page views per month you will pay approx. $30.
